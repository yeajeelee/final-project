Hi! My name is Yeajee and here's my coffee journey.

I first started drinking coffee since COVID when dalgona coffee became viral on TikTok.

Once I got into college, I started drinking coffee for energy.

I began drinking more coffee as a way to hangout with friends.

When my mom opened her boba shop, I began drinking lattes during my opening shifts.

Now when I travel, I look for unique and popular coffee shops to find the best coffee.

Now I drink coffee every day for the taste, comfort, and energy.
