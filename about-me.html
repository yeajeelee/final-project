My Coffee Journey

I first started drinking coffee since COVID when “dalgona coffee” — a whipped sweet coffee — became viral on TikTok.

I began drinking more coffee as a way to hang out with friends.

Once I got into college, I started drinking coffee as a way to get energy.

My mom opened her own boba shop that served coffee, and I began drinking lattes during my opening shifts.

I began drinking coffee every day for both the taste and energy.
